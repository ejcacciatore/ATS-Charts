Key Insights

Market Dominance: IntelligentCross (INCR) and UBS ATS (UBSA) dominate across multiple metrics, consistently ranking #1 or #2 in shares, notional, and trade volume.
Tier Stratification: Clear separation between top-tier venues (ranks 1-5), mid-tier (ranks 6-15), and long-tail venues (ranks 20+), with top 5 capturing over 55% of total market share.
Metric Correlation: Venues ranking highly in shares typically rank highly in notional volume and trades, suggesting consolidated liquidity pools rather than specialized niches.
Symbol Diversity: Larger venues show greater symbol diversity (300-1000 symbols), while smaller venues focus on 50-200 symbols, indicating market segmentation strategies.
