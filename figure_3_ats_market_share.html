Key Findings

Market Concentration: Top 5 venues capture 51.4% of total volume (IntelligentCross 16.1%, UBS ATS 13.0%, Sigma X2 8.6%, Level ATS 7.6%, Instinct X 6.1%), while remaining 28 venues compete for 48.6%.
Innovation Premium: Structural innovation (Tier 1) commands 31.4% of market volume despite representing only 9 venues, suggesting market preference for genuinely differentiated matching protocols.
Segmentation Dominance: Tier 2 sophisticated segmentation venues control 42.3% of volume, the largest share among all tiers, indicating institutions value quantitatively-measured adverse selection protection over pure structural innovation.
Long Tail Challenge: Combined Tiers 3+4 represent only 26.3% of volume across 16 venues, validating the fragmentation and transparency gap thesis—these venues struggle to differentiate and capture meaningful market share.
Competitive Dynamics: Unlike exchange markets with winner-take-all characteristics, ATS competition remains distributed—no single venue exceeds 20% market share, suggesting ongoing value from venue diversity despite concentration trends.
Methodology Notes: Venues classified based on operational structure, matching methodology, and degree of innovation beyond standard continuous midpoint engines. Classifications validated against Form ATS-N disclosures and confirmed operational characteristics. Data period: July 29 - September 15, 2025 (7 weeks). Total volume analyzed: 63.7 billion shares across 33 active NMS Stock ATSs.
